Joondalup Festival’s 2026 program invites you through the portal and into a surreal world of wonder. It’s time to embrace the unusual.

Ignite Your

Festival Spirit

Journey to the heart of Perth’s northern suburbs, where nature’s beauty and human creativity collide in perfect harmony. Let your curiosity guide you through 16 days of wonder—from ancient storytelling and waterborne spectacles to immersive art and electrifying performances. This is where unforgettable experiences await.

Acknowledgement of Country

The City of Joondalup acknowledges the Traditional Custodians of this land, the Whadjuk people of the Noongar nation.

We recognise the enduring culture of the Noongar people and pay our respects to Elders past and present, as well as all Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander peoples.
